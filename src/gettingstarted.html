Getting Started

Digi Dali is a digital art platform on which you can distort images and anonymize faces. It is perfect for creating an anonymous profile picture or greeting card.
You can upload your images directly, or take a selfie and work with that. An edge detection can be applied to the image, and those edges can be approximated and replaced with straight line segments to enable the englaving process (laser engraving process).
Of course you can download your image anytime. You can also carve it as a line drawing using a laser beam into a 10x10 cm wooden board. For a cost of 10 Euros (or the equivalent in your currency) you receive your englaved wood in the mail (incl. shipping).
Receive your englaving and be happy.
